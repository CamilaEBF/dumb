PYRAMIDZ

As ripped from Neopets

Instructions:

Your objective is to clear the pyramid, which is made of a random subset of an ordinary 52-card playing deck. Cards are represented by characters that designate their rank and suit (e.g. 2oH is 2 of Hearts). Cards can be used by stacking the cards on a pile at (the top card is shown under "Current Card"). A card is allowed to be stacked if it is distance one (±1) away from the Current Card. If there is no usable card, you may generate a new Current Card by clicking the "Draw" button. The "Draw" button takes cards from the remainder of the deck that is not in the pyramid.

Since I have made 0 efforts to ensure that the hidden cards are useful to you, this game is usually not winnable. Enjoy.
