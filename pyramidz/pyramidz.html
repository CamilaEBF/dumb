PYRAMIDZ

As ripped from Neopets

Instructions:

Your objective is to clear the pyramid, which is made of a random subset of an ordinary 52-card playing deck, by stacking the cards on this pile at the top. A card is allowed to be stacked if it is distance one away from the card currently displayed on the top of the pile.

Since I have made 0 efforts to ensure that the hidden cards are useful to you, this game is usually not winnable. Enjoy.
